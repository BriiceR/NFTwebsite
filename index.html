THE PROJECT

For all lovers of plants and technology, “The Simplified Plant” offers you to acquire one or more plants that you will not have to take care of, no water, no sun, no pruning...

Buy, sell, exchange everything is possible with store 3.0, evolve your collection and join the community.

You can also display your plant in your favourite metaverse space.
